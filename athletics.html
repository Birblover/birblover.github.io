Athletics Facilities

Veritas University is home to a range of state-of-the-art athletics facilities, including:

  • The Veritas University Arena, a 5,000-seat multi-purpose arena that is home to our men's and women's basketball teams and serves as a venue for concerts and other events
  • The Veritas University Field House, a 50,000-square-foot indoor facility that includes a track, basketball courts, and other sports courts
  • The Veritas University Aquatic Center, a 25-yard pool that is home to our men's and women's swimming and diving teams
  • The Veritas University Tennis Center, a complex of 16 outdoor courts and four indoor courts that is home to our men's and women's tennis teams
  • The Veritas University Soccer Stadium, a 2,500-seat stadium that is home to our men's and women's soccer teams
  • The Veritas University Baseball Stadium, a 1,500-seat stadium that is home to our baseball team
  • The Veritas University Softball Stadium, a 1,000-seat stadium that is home to our softball team

For more information about our athletics facilities, including hours of operation and rental information, please visit the athletics facilities page.
