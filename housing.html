Housing at Veritas University

Veritas University is committed to providing a vibrant and welcoming living and learning environment for our students. We offer a range of housing options to suit the needs and preferences of our diverse student body. All full-time undergraduate students are required to live on campus for their first four years at Veritas, unless they meet certain criteria for exemption. Please see the housing exemption policy for more information.

Residence Halls

Veritas University has several residence halls to choose from, each with its own unique character and community. Our residence halls offer a variety of room types, including traditional double rooms, triple rooms, and suite-style rooms. All rooms are furnished and include basic cable and internet access. Laundry facilities and common areas are located in each building, and each residence hall has a live-in resident assistant to provide support and assistance to students.

For more information about the different types of rooms available in our residence halls, please visit the room types page. For a complete list of all of our residence hall options, please visit the residence halls page.

Apartment-Style Housing

Veritas University also offers apartment-style housing for upperclassmen and graduate students. Our apartment buildings offer furnished one-, two-, and four-bedroom units with private bathrooms and kitchens. Laundry facilities and common areas are located in each building, and there is a live-in resident assistant to provide support and assistance to students. Apartment-style housing is a great option for students who want more independence and a sense of community.

For more information about our apartment-style housing options, please visit the apartment housing page. Here you will find details on each of our apartment buildings, including floor plans, amenities, and location. You will also find information on the application process and requirements for apartment-style housing.

Application Process

To apply for housing at Veritas University, you must:

  • Submit a completed housing application and all required supporting documents
  • Pay the non-refundable housing deposit
  • Complete the housing contract

The housing application and all required documents are available on the housing application page. The housing deposit is due upon acceptance to Veritas University and is applied towards your housing charges for the academic year. The housing contract is a legally binding document that outlines your responsibilities as a resident of Veritas University housing. It is important that you read and understand the terms of the contract before signing it.

If you have any questions about the housing application process, please don't hesitate to contact us. You can reach the housing office at housing@veritas.edu or (555) 555-5555.
