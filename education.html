School of Education at Veritas University

Welcome to the School of Education at Veritas University! We are a top-ranked school of education, committed to preparing the next generation of teachers, counselors, and educational leaders. Our faculty are experts in their fields, and our small class sizes and personalized approach to education ensure that our students receive the best possible education.

Academic Programs

The School of Education at Veritas University offers a wide range of academic programs at the undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral levels. Our programs are designed to prepare students for successful careers in education, counseling, and leadership. We offer programs in a variety of areas, including:

  • Elementary Education
  • Secondary Education
  • Special Education
  • Counseling
  • Educational Leadership

For more information about our academic programs, please visit the academic programs page.

Faculty

The School of Education at Veritas University is home to a talented and dedicated faculty of educators, researchers, and practitioners. Our faculty are experts in their fields and are committed to providing a high-quality education to our students. They are also active in research and have published widely in their areas of expertise. You can find more information about our faculty and their research on the faculty page.

Field Experience

Field experience is an important part of the education programs at Veritas University. We believe that hands-on experience is essential for preparing our students for careers in education, counseling, and leadership. All of our education programs include field experience components, where students have the opportunity to work with real students in real classrooms. For more information about field experience at Veritas University, please visit the field experience page.

Contact Us

You can also schedule a virtual appointment with an education admissions advisor by visiting the admissions website.
