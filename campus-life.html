Campus Life at Veritas University

Residence Halls

Veritas University offers a range of housing options for students, including traditional residence halls, suite-style apartments, and off-campus housing. All residence halls are equipped with high-speed internet and basic cable service, and many offer additional amenities such as laundry facilities and common areas for studying and socializing.

To apply for housing, please visit the Housing page on our website for more information and to complete the online application.

Student Activities

Veritas University offers a wide variety of extracurricular activities for students to get involved on campus and in the community. Our student organizations cover a range of interests, including academic, cultural, social, recreational, and service-oriented groups.

To learn more about student organizations and how to get involved, please visit the Student Activities page on our website.

Dining Services

Veritas University offers a range of dining options on campus, including a cafeteria, a food court, and several coffee shops and snack bars. We offer a variety of meal plans to suit different needs and preferences, and all of our dining options feature a range of healthy and tasty options.

For more information about dining services, please visit the Dining Services page on our website.

Athletics

Veritas University is home to a wide range of athletics programs, including football, basketball, baseball, soccer, tennis, golf, and more. Our teams compete at the NCAA Division I level and have a strong tradition of success on and off the field.

To learn more about our athletics programs, visit the Athletics page on our website. You can find schedules, scores, and more information about our teams and facilities.

Health and Wellness

Veritas University is committed to supporting the health and wellness of our students. We offer a range of services and resources to help students maintain their physical, mental, and emotional well-being, including a student health center, counseling services, and wellness programs.

To learn more about health and wellness at Veritas University, visit the Health and Wellness page on our website.

Transportation

Veritas University offers a range of transportation options for students, including a shuttle service, bike-sharing program, and discounted public transportation passes. We also offer a carpool program for students who wish to share rides to and from campus.

For more information about transportation options at Veritas University, visit the Transportation page on our website.
