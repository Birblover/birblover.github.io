College of Science at Veritas University

The College of Science at Veritas University is dedicated to providing our students with a rigorous and comprehensive education in the scientific disciplines. Our faculty are experts in their fields, and are committed to conducting cutting-edge research and sharing their knowledge with our students. We offer a wide range of programs at the undergraduate and graduate levels, including:

  • Biology (B.S., M.S., Ph.D.)
  • Chemistry (B.S., M.S., Ph.D.)
  • Computer Science (B.S., M.S., Ph.D.)
  • Environmental Science (B.S., M.S.)
  • Mathematics (B.S., M.S., Ph.D.)
  • Physics (B.S., M.S., Ph.D.)
  • Psychology (B.S., M.S., Ph.D.)

Our programs are designed to give students a strong foundation in the principles of science, as well as the opportunity to specialize in a particular area of interest. We also offer a number of research opportunities for students, both on campus and at external research institutions, which allow them to gain valuable hands-on experience and make meaningful contributions to the scientific community.

In addition to our academic programs, the College of Science at Veritas University is committed to promoting diversity and inclusion. We believe that a diverse and inclusive community is essential for the advancement of science, and we strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment for all of our students.

If you are interested in pursuing a degree in the sciences at Veritas University, we encourage you to visit our academics website or contact the College of Science at science@veritas.edu or (555) 555-5555. You can also schedule a virtual appointment with an academic advisor by visiting the academics website.
