Dining Services at Veritas University

Veritas University is committed to providing a range of dining options for our students that are convenient, affordable, and delicious. We have several dining facilities on campus, each with a different atmosphere and menu to suit different tastes and preferences.

Dining Facilities

Our dining facilities include:

  • The Main Dining Hall - a large, all-you-can-eat facility with a variety of hot and cold menu options, including vegetarian and vegan options
  • The Caf√© - a casual dining option with sandwiches, salads, soups, and other grab-and-go items
  • The Grill - a fast food option with burgers, fries, chicken sandwiches, and other classic favorites
  • The Market - a convenience store with snack foods, drinks, and other essentials

For more information about our dining facilities, including hours of operation, menus, and special events, please visit the dining facilities page.

Meal Plans

Veritas University offers a range of meal plans to suit different needs and preferences. Our meal plans include:

  • The Unlimited Plan - allows you to eat as much as you want at any of our dining facilities
  • The 10-Meal Plan - provides 10 meals per week at the Main Dining Hall, with the option to purchase additional meals or guest passes
  • The 20-Meal Plan - provides 20 meals per week at the Main Dining Hall, with the option to purchase additional meals or guest passes
  • The Block Plan - provides a certain number of meals that can be used at any of our dining facilities, with the option to purchase additional meals or guest passes

For more information about our meal plans, including prices and how to sign up, please visit the meal plans page.

Special Diets

Veritas University is committed to providing a range of options for students with special dietary needs. We offer vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other options at all of our dining facilities. If you have any other special dietary needs, please let us know and we will do our best to accommodate you.

For more information about special diets at Veritas University, please visit the special diets page or contact the dining services office at dining-services@veritas.edu or (555) 555-5555.
