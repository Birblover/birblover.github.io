Graduate Admissions at Veritas University

Application Process

To apply for graduate admission at Veritas University, you will need to complete the following steps:

  1. Submit an online application form, along with a non-refundable application fee.
  2. Submit official transcripts from all colleges and universities attended.
  3. Submit GRE or GMAT scores, if applicable.
  4. Submit a personal essay or statement of purpose.
  5. Arrange for one or more letters of recommendation, if applicable.

Admission Requirements

To be considered for admission to Veritas University, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Hold a bachelor's degree from an accredited institution.
  • Have a minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale.
  • Meet any program-specific admission requirements, if applicable.

Tuition and Financial Aid

Veritas University is committed to making higher education accessible to all qualified students, regardless of their financial circumstances. We offer a range of financial aid options, including scholarships, grants, loans, and assistantships. To apply for financial aid, you will need to complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA).

For more information about tuition and financial aid at Veritas University, please visit our financial aid website or contact the financial aid office at finaid@veritas.edu or (555) 555-5555. You can also schedule a virtual appointment with a financial aid advisor by visiting the financial aid website.

International Students

Veritas University is proud to welcome students from all around the world. International students must meet the same admission requirements as domestic students, but there are a few additional steps that you will need to take. For example, you will need to provide proof of English proficiency, either through TOEFL or IELTS scores, or by completing the English as a Second Language (ESL) program at Veritas University. You will also need to apply for a student visa, which requires a few additional documents and fees.

For more information about the admission process for international students at Veritas University, please visit the international students website or contact the international students office at intlstudents@veritas.edu or (555) 555-5555. You can also schedule a virtual appointment with an international student advisor by visiting the international students website.
