Health & Wellness

At Veritas University, we are committed to the health and wellness of our students. We have a range of offices and resources available to support you in maintaining your physical and mental health while you are on campus.

Student Health Center

The Veritas University Student Health Center is a full-service medical clinic that is available to all enrolled students. Our team of healthcare professionals is here to help you with everything from routine check-ups to urgent care needs. The Student Health Center also offers counseling and mental health services for students.

Counseling Services

Veritas University Counseling Services is here to support you with your emotional and mental health needs. Our team of licensed counselors is available to help you with a wide range of issues, including stress, anxiety, depression, relationship problems, and more. Counseling services are free and confidential for all enrolled students.

Wellness Programs

Veritas University offers a range of wellness programs and activities to help students maintain a healthy lifestyle. Some examples include yoga classes, meditation sessions, nutrition workshops, and outdoor adventures. Check out the student activities page for more information about what's available.
