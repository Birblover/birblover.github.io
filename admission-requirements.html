Admission Requirements at Veritas University

Veritas University is a highly selective institution that is committed to providing a rigorous and challenging education to qualified students. Our admission requirements are designed to ensure that our students have the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed in their studies at Veritas. We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds and encourage you to apply if you meet the following requirements:

Undergraduate Admission Requirements

In order to be considered for undergraduate admission at Veritas University, you must:

  • Submit a completed application form and all required supporting documents
  • Have a high school diploma or equivalent from an accredited institution
  • Submit official transcripts from all high schools and colleges attended
  • Take the ACT or SAT (unless you have completed a bachelor's degree or higher) and submit your scores
  • Have a minimum GPA of 3.5
  • Submit a personal essay and at least one letter of recommendation

In addition to these general requirements, some programs may have additional admission requirements. Please see the academic programs page for more information about specific program requirements.

Veritas University uses a holistic review process when considering applicants for admission. We look at a variety of factors, including academic performance, standardized test scores, extracurricular activities, leadership and community service, as well as the personal essay and letter of recommendation. We want to get to know you as a whole person, not just your grades and test scores.

Graduate Admission Requirements

In order to be considered for graduate admission at Veritas University, you must:

  • Submit a completed application form and all required supporting documents
  • Have a bachelor's degree or higher from an accredited institution
  • Submit official transcripts from all colleges and universities attended
  • Have a minimum GPA of 3.5
  • Take the appropriate graduate admissions exam (e.g. GMAT, GRE, etc.) and submit your scores
  • Submit a personal statement and at least two letters of recommendation

In addition to these general requirements, some programs may have additional admission requirements. Please see the academic programs page for more information about specific program requirements.

Veritas University is committed to academic rigor and expects a high level of achievement from its graduate students. We will carefully review your application materials to ensure that you have the necessary skills and knowledge to succeed in your chosen program of study.

International Admission Requirements

In addition to the general admission requirements for Veritas University, international students must also:

  • Provide proof of English proficiency, either through TOEFL or IELTS scores, or by completing the English as a Second Language (ESL) program
  • Submit a Financial Declaration form and proof of financial support
  • Obtain a student visa

For more information about the admission process for international students at Veritas University, please visit the international admissions page.

Transfer Admission Requirements

In order to be considered for transfer admission at Veritas University, you must:

  • Submit a completed application form and all required supporting documents
  • Have a minimum of 30 college credits completed
  • Submit official transcripts from all colleges and universities attended
  • Have a minimum GPA of 3.5
  • Submit a transfer evaluation form and course descriptions for all courses taken at previous institutions

In addition to these general requirements, some programs may have additional admission requirements. Please see the academic programs page for more information about specific program requirements.

Veritas University is committed to academic excellence and will carefully review your transcripts and course descriptions to ensure that you have the necessary skills and knowledge to succeed in your chosen program of study. Transfer credit is granted on a course-by-course basis and is subject to review by the appropriate academic department.

If you have any questions about the admission requirements at Veritas University, please don't hesitate to contact us. You can reach the admissions office at admissions@veritas.edu or (555) 555-5555.
