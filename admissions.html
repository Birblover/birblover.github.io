Admissions at Veritas University

Undergraduate Admissions

Veritas University is a selective institution, and we review each application carefully. We consider a variety of factors, including academic achievement, test scores, essays, letters of recommendation, and extracurricular activities.

To apply for undergraduate admission, please submit the following materials:

  • Application form
  • Official high school transcript
  • SAT or ACT scores
  • Essay
  • Two letters of recommendation

For more information about undergraduate admissions, please visit our Undergraduate Admissions page.

Graduate Admissions

Veritas University is dedicated to providing high-quality graduate education to students who are committed to advancing their knowledge and skills in their field of study. Our graduate programs are designed to challenge and support students as they pursue their academic and professional goals.

To apply for graduate admission, please submit the following materials:

  • Application form
  • Official undergraduate transcript
  • Resume or CV
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Writing sample

For more information about graduate admissions, please visit our Graduate Admissions page.

International Admissions

Veritas University is proud to welcome international students from around the world. We offer a wide range of programs and support services to help international students succeed in their studies and fully engage in campus life.

To apply for international admissions, please submit the following materials:

  • Application form
  • Official transcripts
  • English language proficiency test scores
  • Financial documents showing sufficient funds for tuition and living expenses
  • Copy of passport

For more information about international admissions, please visit our International Admissions page.

Transfer Admissions

Veritas University is committed to helping students who are transferring from other colleges and universities succeed in their studies. We offer a wide range of programs and support services to help transfer students adjust to life at Veritas and fully engage in campus life.

To apply for transfer admissions, please submit the following materials:

  • Application form
  • Official transcripts from all colleges and universities attended
  • Essay

For more information about transfer admissions, please visit our Transfer Admissions page.
