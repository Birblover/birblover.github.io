College of Liberal Arts at Veritas University

The College of Liberal Arts at Veritas University is dedicated to providing our students with a well-rounded and comprehensive education in the humanities, social sciences, and fine arts. Our faculty are experts in their fields, and are committed to promoting critical thinking, creativity, and cultural awareness. We offer a wide range of programs at the undergraduate and graduate levels, including:

  • Art (B.A.)
  • Creative Writing (B.A., M.F.A.)
  • Economics (B.A., M.A., Ph.D.)
  • English (B.A., M.A., Ph.D.)
  • History (B.A., M.A., Ph.D.)
  • Music (B.A., M.A., Ph.D.)
  • Musical Theater (B.A.)
  • Philosophy (B.A., M.A., Ph.D.)
  • Political Science (B.A., M.A., Ph.D.)
  • Sociology (B.A., M.A., Ph.D.)

Our programs are designed to give students a strong foundation in the principles of the liberal arts, as well as the opportunity to specialize in a particular area of interest. We also offer a number of study abroad and internship opportunities for students, which allow them to gain valuable real-world experience and broaden their cultural horizons.

In addition to our academic programs, the College of Liberal Arts at Veritas University is committed to promoting diversity and inclusion. We believe that a diverse and inclusive community is essential for the advancement of the liberal arts, and we strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment for all of our students.

If you are interested in pursuing a degree in the liberal arts at Veritas University, we encourage you to visit our academics website or contact the College of Liberal Arts at liberalarts@veritas.edu or (555) 555-5555. You can also schedule a virtual appointment with an academic advisor by visiting the academics website.
